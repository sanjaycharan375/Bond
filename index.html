The Process

Simple to start.
Built to last.

A Bond takes two minutes to create and a lifetime to develop.

01
Create your account

Sign up and tell Bond about yourself — your communication style, how you handle conflict, what matters most to you emotionally.

02
Invite your person

Send a unique link to your partner, best friend, or family member. They join and complete their own profile independently.

03
AI analyses your Bond

Bond maps your emotional dynamics, identifies real strengths and friction points, and builds a relationship intelligence profile unique to you two.

04
Get daily tasks

Every day you receive a new micro-task — specific to your pair. Not generic advice. Actions that shift real patterns over time.
